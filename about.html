It all starts with the idea.

Created by a team of students from the Woodlands Secondary School, hack::peel was, as an executive put it, "the product of a desire to make hackathons and their opportunites a universal resource, accessible to all". Without the hard work of our team and the generosity of our sponsors, this amazing event wouldn't be possible!
